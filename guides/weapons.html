Classes and Weapons Guide

Master the art of combat with our comprehensive guide to classes and weapons in The Quinfall

Dual Axe

Warriors who expertly wield two great axes, known for delivering powerful, crushing physical attacks that can overwhelm enemies with devastating blows.

Blood Master

Masters of inflicting wounds and bleeding effects, these warriors weaken enemies over time, causing them to bleed to death.

Stormblade

Swift and devastating warriors who charge into battle, delivering powerful and rapid attacks to their enemies.

Dagger

Swift warriors who skillfully wield fast and sharp daggers, known for delivering quick and devastating physical attacks.

Deathblade

Masters of swift strikes, delivering fast and sustained damage with superior speed advantage.

Shadow Stalker

Stealthy assassins who use poison and invisibility to strike from the shadows.

War Hammer

Powerful warriors who wield massive war hammers, combining strength with tactical support abilities.

Strengthener

Support specialists who enhance team performance, defense, and offensive capabilities.

Slow Devastator

Masters of powerful, slower strikes that deal massive damage to enemies.

Staff

Magical warriors who wield mystical staffs, capable of casting various spells for both offense and support.

Frost Mage

Masters of ice magic who slow and freeze enemies with powerful frost spells.

Fire Mage

Specialists in devastating area-of-effect fire damage and magical attacks.

Life Staff

Healers who channel life energy through their staffs, providing crucial support and healing to allies.

Healer

Dedicated healers who maintain and revitalize the team's health during battle.

Magic Warden

Hybrid support who combine magical attacks with defensive buffs and enemy debuffs.

Crossbow

Ranged specialists who excel at dealing precise damage from a distance with their crossbows.

Sharpshooter

Expert marksmen who specialize in high-damage precision shots from long range.

Assault Master

Rapid-fire specialists who excel at dealing quick successive damage to enemies.

Long Sword

Warriors who master the art of wielding large and heavy longswords with devastating effect.

Strongsword

Two-handed sword masters who combine skill and strength in powerful attacks.

Devastator

Specialists in stunning and devastating attacks, particularly effective against vulnerable targets.

Spear

Masters of reach and precision who excel at controlling the battlefield with their spears.

Lancer

Aggressive warriors who use piercing attacks and lead the charge in battle.

Valkyrie

Versatile fighters who combine offensive capabilities with battlefield control.

Bow

Agile archers who form bonds with nature and excel at precise ranged combat.

Hunter

Nature-attuned warriors who combine beast companionship with stealthy combat.

Critical Archer

Precision specialists who excel at dealing high damage through calculated shots.
