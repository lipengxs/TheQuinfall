World Map System

Quinfall provides players with a detailed map and a convenient mini-map for those who wish to explore the vast world. Here are the key features of the map system.

Mini-Map

  • The mini-map is located in one corner of the screen and displays your character, surroundings, and nearby important points.
  • You can personalize mini-map features, zoom in and out, and track your character's location from the settings menu.
  • The mini-map is a significant aid during short-distance journeys, completing quests, and being aware of nearby dangers.

Large Map

  • Access the large map by pressing the "M" key. This map offers a broad perspective and covers a larger area.
  • On the large map, you can obtain a detailed view of symbols, icons, and important locations. This is valuable for long-distance journeys and general exploration.
  • Custom icons indicate quests, trade points, dungeons, and other crucial features, assisting players in reaching their objectives.

Map Settings

  • Map settings allow players to customize their map view. Adjusting icon size, visibility, and the overall detail level of the map is possible.
  • While navigating the map, you can easily identify nearby resources, biomes, and significant locations.

Quinfall's map system is designed to enable players to explore the world and navigate effectively. While the mini-map guides players in daily activities, the large map helps them better understand and explore the gaming world together.
